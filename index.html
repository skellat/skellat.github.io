Your Boutique Multi-Platform Media Arts Shop

Erie Looking Productions is based in Ashtabula, Ohio. We are a boutique multi-platform media arts shop available for contract work. From work in LaTeX2e to audio production to video editing, we provide unique solutions for you. Examples of audio works we have created can be found online at the Internet Archive.

If you're looking for the blog of Stephen Michael Kellat, please head over instead to Coyote Works which is powered using ikiwiki.

If you're looking for The Interim Edgewood Stratagem, our current newsletter, please sign-up at tinyletter.com.

Services During the COVID-19 Crisis

Presently our services are limited during the current pandemic crisis. We have moved the listing of services to a separate page for reference. If we are able to help you remotely we most certainly will. Please contact us for more details.

Works in Progress

Our works have changed thanks to the COVID-19 crisis. The discussion of what we are doing has been moved to a separate page for easier review.

Support Your Local Small Business

As a sole proprietorship we are not part of the initial gold rush to seek funding from the Small Business Administration's loan program under the CARES Act in response to the COVID-19 crisis. We are on our own until at least April 10th and are possibly too small to qualify. We are sustained by business instead of federal largess.

To Reach Us

For more information as well as price quotes, please contact us via Telegram or via Skype. Our rather secondary presence on Facebook can be found at https://www.facebook.com/erielooking/ and we generally prefer to operate outside the hegemonic realm of the House of Zuckerberg.
